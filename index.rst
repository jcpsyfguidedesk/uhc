Welcome to UHC Online Services
------------------------------

**UHC** online portal is designed to help users
access healthcare-related services easily and securely. Through
**activate.uhc.com**, users can activate their UHC account, register online,
log in securely, and check balance information anytime.

This portal supports hospitals, healthcare providers, and patients by offering
a digital platform that reduces paperwork and improves accessibility.


How to Activate UHC Online
--------------------------

UHC account activation is a simple and secure process.

Steps to activate UHC online:

1. Visit **activate.uhc.com**.
2. Click on **UHC Activate Online**.
3. Enter your UHC ID or registered details.
4. Verify using OTP sent to your mobile or email.
5. Create a secure password.
6. Submit the form to complete activation.

After activation, your UHC services will be available online.


How to Register for UHC Online
------------------------------

New users can register for **UHC online** by following these steps:

1. Open **activate.uhc.com**.
2. Select **UHC Online Registration**.
3. Enter personal and healthcare details.
4. Provide contact information.
5. Set login credentials.
6. Submit the registration form.

Once registered, proceed with UHC activation.


How to Login to UHC Portal
--------------------------

Registered UHC users can log in securely:

1. Go to **activate.uhc.com**.
2. Click **UHC Login**.
3. Enter username or UHC ID.
4. Enter password.
5. Click **Login** to access your dashboard.

Use **Forgot Password** if required.


How to Check UHC Balance Online
-------------------------------

UHC users can check balance and service details online:

1. Log in to your UHC account.
2. Navigate to **UHC Account / Balance** section.
3. View available balance and usage history.
4. Download statements if needed.

This helps users track healthcare benefits easily.


Benefits of UHC Online Portal
-----------------------------

- Universal Health Coverage access
- Secure UHC account management
- 24/7 online availability
- Easy balance checking
- Patient-friendly interface
